How we work

Four steps, no surprises.

Every project follows the same rhythm. You'll know what's happening this week, next week, and the week after.

01

Walkthrough

We meet at your property, listen, take photos and measurements. About an hour. No pressure, no quote-on-the-spot pressure tactics.

02

Design

You get a written estimate and, for design jobs, a plan with plant list and phasing. We review it together until it fits.

03

Build

One crew, one foreman, fixed timeline. We protect the lawn, clean up daily, and keep you in the loop with weekly photos.

04

Maintain

If you signed on for weekly care, the same crew handles ongoing visits. Same day every week. Same standard.
